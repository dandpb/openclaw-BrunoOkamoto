# ✈️ Use Case: Travel Research and Budgeting

> Complete travel research, budgets, and comparisons.

## What it does

Researches and compares travel options:
- Hotels and resorts with prices
- Flights and transfers
- Optimized itineraries
- Estimated total budget
- Best seasons and deals

## Prompt

```
I want to plan a trip to [DESTINATION].

Details:
- [NUMBER] people
- [NUMBER] nights
- Flexible dates: [MONTH/PERIOD]
- Maximum budget: R$ [AMOUNT]
- Preferences: [RESORT HOTEL / AIRBNB / BUDGET]

Do a deep search and deliver:
1. **3 accommodation options** — with price, rating, and pros/cons
2. **Detailed budget** — accommodation + flights + food + activities
3. **Suggested itinerary** — day by day with activities
4. **Best time to go** — to save money or for the best weather
5. **Local tips** — things you researched that tourists don't know

Compare all 3 options side by side in a table.
```

## Tip

Works for any budget research — not just travel. Use it to compare suppliers, tools, services, etc.
