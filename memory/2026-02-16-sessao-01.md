### 3. Brainstorm: "Self-executing" Course
- Idea: member's OpenClaw runs the course (automated PRDs)
- Initial proposal: "OpenClaw on Rails" — member says "apply module X"
- Pros and cons analysis:

**Pros:**
- Meta-differentiator (learn OpenClaw using OpenClaw)
- Built-in proof of concept
- Scalable support
- Viral marketing

**Critical cons:**
- Chicken-and-egg paradox (module 1 cannot be automated)
- Learning paradox (does everything, learns nothing)
- Different environments = breakage
- Security risk (PRDs that modify the system)
- PRDs easy to pirate

**Decision/Recommendation:**
- GUIDED TUTOR model > Auto-pilot
- Agent explains + asks for confirmation + executes + validates
- Member understands the why, not just the how
